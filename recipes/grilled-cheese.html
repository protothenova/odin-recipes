Grilled Cheese

Description

A grilled cheese sandwich is a classic food enjoyed by many, and requires very little skill and few ingredients to make.

This recipe will show you, step by step, how to make the perfect grilled cheese sandwich that will leave you coming back for more!

Ingredients

  • White bread slices, however most types of bread will work in it's place
  • Sliced cheddar cheese, however you can use American cheese or Colby Jack if you prefer, like I do.
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted.
  5. Enjoy!
