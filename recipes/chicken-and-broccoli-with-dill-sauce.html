Chicken and Broccoli with Dill Sauce

Description

This delicious recipe makes broccoli, chicken, and a creamy dill sauce, all using the same pan!

Add a side of rice and you have a complete meal that is both healthy and filling!

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves (6 ounces each)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cups fresh broccoli florets
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon snipped fresh dill
  • 1 cup 2% milk

Steps

  1. Sprinkle chicken with garlic salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat; brown chicken on both sides. Remove from pan.
  2. Add broccoli and broth to same skillet; bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer simmer, covered, until broccoli is just tender, 3-5 minutes.
  3. Using a slotted spoon, remove broccoli from pan, reserving broth. Keep broccoli warm.
  4. In a small bowl, mix flour, dill, and milk until smooth. Stir into broth in pan.
  5. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly; cook and stir until thickened, 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add chicken; cook, covered, over medium heat until a thermometer inserted into chicken reads 165Â°F, 10-12 minutes.
  7. Serve with broccoli and enjoy!
