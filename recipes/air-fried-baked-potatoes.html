Air Fried Baked Potatoes

Description

These crispy baked potatoes take very little effort and taste delicious!

Topped with your choice of toppings, this can make for a very easy lunch.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 large potatoes, scrubbed
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients, and rub potatoes with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Place potatoes in the air fryer basket at 400Â° for 40-50 minutes, depending on potato size.
  3. Once removed from the air fryer, slice potatoes lengthwise. Pinch both sides of each potato, using your hands and forcing potatoes to open, until the fluffy insides start to come out.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon of butter into each potato, top with your desired toppings, and enjoy!
